2.4 Visualization of Data

In the next phase of our exploratory data analysis we will be visualizing the data. In Section 1.4, we discussed some of the ideas of visualization. For the first part, we will be generating visualization to help understand the content of the data.

2.4.1 First Visualizations

In our first visualization we need to try to find key varialbes from the 9 total variables. The first three variables, country, continent, and year are different from the last seven and will have to be dealt with in a different manner.

2.4.1.1 Discussion Ideas:

What are the first steps we should take to understand and visualize the data?

Are we doing reconnaissance or exploration, and what does that mean for the type of visualizations we want to create?
