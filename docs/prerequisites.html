0.3 Prerequisites

WHY USE R what if you don’t want to

These notes make use of the following packages in R knitr (Xie 2017b), rmarkdown (Allaire et al. 2017), fastR (Pruim 2017), Hmisc (Harrell 2018), lattice (Sarkar 2017), vcd (Meyer, Zeileis, and Hornik 2017), ggplot2 (Wickham and Chang 2016), MASS (Ripley 2017), TeachingDemos (Snow 2016), Stat2Data (Lock 2013), car (Fox and Weisberg 2017), DT (Xie 2016),crosstalk (Cheng 2016), leaflet (Cheng, Karambelkar, and Xie 2017), plotly (Sievert et al. 2017).
